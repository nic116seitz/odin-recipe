Daikon Mochi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Daikon
  • Cornstarch 2 Tablespoon
  • Flour 2 Tablespoon
  • Soy Sauce 1 TSP
  • Bunch of Green Onions Chopped
  • Splash of fish sauce

Steps to prepare

  1. Grate the Daikon
  2. Squeeze and drain the daikon of water, until pretty dry
  3. Incorporate the green onion
  4. Incorporate the startch and flour into the grated daikon
  5. Create the daikon mochi patties of the desireed size
  6. Dust the mochi with cornstarch on the outside
  7. Fry the patties after resting for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy :)
